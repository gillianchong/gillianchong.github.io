The Dusun
Boasting a swimming pool, a garden and views of the mountain, The Dusun is located in Seremban and features free WiFi in public areas. Units are fitted with a kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer and bidet. There is also a fridge, stovetop and a kettle. A children's playground can be found at The Dusun, along with a terrace. Palm Mall Seremban is 12 min from the accommodations. The nearest airport is Kuala Lumpur International, 39 min from The Dusun, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
Location: 3437 Kampung Kolam Air Mukim Pantai, 71770 Seremban, Malaysia

Royale Chulan Seremban
Located in the Lake Garden district, Royale Chulan Seremban offers 4-star accommodations with a gym and outdoor pool with an attached spa pool. It features free Wi-Fi and 4 dining options. Royale Chulan Seremban is a 1-hour drive from Kuala Lumpur International Airport and Sepang International Circuit. Royale Chulan Seremban’s rooms feature classic furnishings and comfortable armchairs with ottomans. They are fitted with a safe, satellite TV and ironing facilities. Private bathrooms have a bathtub and separate shower stalls. Guests can lounge by the landscaped outdoor pool. A well-equipped fitness center and tennis courts await those looking to exercise. Car hire and shuttle services are provided. Buffet spreads with international dishes are available at Asiatique, while Han Pi Yuen offers fine Chinese dining and Dim Sum. Other dining options include freshly baked pastries at Sweet Seremban and drinks at Anjung Lounge. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.8 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
Location: Jalan Dato A.S. Dawood, 70100 Seremban, Malaysia

Palm Seremban Hotel
Operating a 24-hour front desk, Palm Seremban Hotel offers elegant and comfortable accommodations with free WiFi access in the entire property. Located beside KPJ Specialist Hospital, it features a business center and provides free parking on site. Fitted with soundproofed walls, air-conditioned rooms include a wardrobe, in-room safe, ironing facilities and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Offering shower facility, the private bathroom also comes with hairdryer, slippers, a spray bidet and free toiletries. At Palm Seremban Hotel, friendly staff is fluently-conversed in Malay, Chinese and English. Luggage storage, laundry and concierge services are available, while meeting/banquet facilities can be arranged upon request. The property houses a restaurant serving a menu of tasty à la carte local dishes. Strategically located, Palm Seremban Hotel is integrated with Palm Mall, the largest shopping center in Negeri Sembilan. Port Dickson beach is 30 minutes’ drive away, while Kuala Lumpur International Airport is accessible within a 29 mi drive. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.8 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
Location: Jalan Sungei Ujong, 70200 Seremban, Malaysia
