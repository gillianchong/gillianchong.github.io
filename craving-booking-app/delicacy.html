Onn Kee Satay
This family run business has been operated for more than 50 years in seremban. The current lady boss inherited the necessary skill and technique for making delicious satay from her father. Everything here is homemade including satay sauce.
Cuisine : Chinese
Business Hour : 07:00 pm - 12:00 am
Location : Gerai 22, Food Court Huat Hing (Formerly Known as 2020 Food Court) ,Jalan Temiang, 70200 Seremban, Negeri Sembilan Malaysia.
Tel No : 019-2849106
Kedai Makanan Seremban Seafood Village
Kedai Makanan Seremban Seafood Village is famous of their signature dish Seremban Grilled Crabs or Seremban Baked Crab (with Pau). Their Kung Pao Mantis Shrimp and Kam Heong La La are surprisingly cheap and tasty. In overall, this restaurant serves decent seafood at reasonable prices despite being famous and is only about 70km driving distance from KL.
Cuisine : Seafood
Business Hour : 11:00 am - 01:00 am
Location : No. 3720-3724, Jalan Tuanku Munawir, 70200 Seremban, Negeri Sembilan Malaysia.
Tel No : 06-7641260
Restoran Selera Nogori
Restoran Selera Nogori sebuah restoran yang menawarkan pelbagai jenis makanan tradisional negeri Sembilan dan makanan melayu. Restoran ini sangat terkenal bukan dikalangan orang setempat malahan turut menjadi destinasi pilihan pelancong. Terdapat kira-kira 30 jenis masakan yang sedap untuk dipilih oleh setiap pelanggan.
Cuisine : Asian, Buffet, Local, Malay
Business Hour : (Monday - Sunday) 7.00 am - 5.00 pm
Location : 602&603, Jalan Haruan 4/8, Oakland Commercial Centre, 70300 Seremban, Negeri Sembilan.
Tel No : 013-253 3334
